What was the project?

This was the final project of the semester. We had to do another design sprint and create a native app that makes study life easier for KEA students. We had to do another design sprint. My group decided to create an organisational task application.

What did I learn?

It was good to get back to doing a sprint, and it seemed to go a lot smoother this time. I knew what to expect from the sprint which was helpful.

We used our team canvas a lot this time around, it was nice to be able to refer back to it from time to time.

We did 1 on 1 user tests on the first day of the sprint, which made us rethink the idea of the app and change it from a notetaking app to a task app.

What would I change today?

The project was finished very late on the 4th day of the sprint so we didn't have a chance to test the prototype before the pitch. This would of course have been useful beforehand. However, we got very good feedback and although we didn't win a star from the teacher the heatmap showed that we got a lot of votes for user friendliness and the idea behind the app.
